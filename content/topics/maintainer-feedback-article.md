# Write about what makes maintainer feedback useful

This topic should become an article about contribution retention, not just communication etiquette.

## Questions the article should answer

- What kinds of maintainer replies reduce hesitation?
- What makes review feedback feel actionable instead of dismissive?
- How does response speed matter compared with response quality?
- Why do some contributors disappear after the first interaction?

## What the article should connect

The final piece should connect feedback style to practical outcomes:

- contributor confidence
- repeat participation
- clarity of next steps
- whether a repository feels worth returning to

## What to avoid

- generic advice like "be nice"
- treating feedback as a tone issue only
- ignoring the connection between review quality and contributor retention
