# Compare documentation platforms for small teams

This topic asks for a guide, not a roundup.

## What the final piece should help a reader decide

The guide should help a small team answer questions like:

- Do we need versioned docs?
- How much theming or custom code can we realistically maintain?
- Is Markdown-only authoring enough for us?
- Which navigation and search needs matter most for our size?

## What the guide should include

- a clear team profile at the top
- the criteria used to compare options
- concrete tradeoffs instead of marketing phrases
- a recommendation pattern such as "choose this kind of platform if..."

## What to avoid

- feature matrices without explanation
- pretending there is one universal winner
- praising tools without saying what they cost to maintain
